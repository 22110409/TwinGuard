What Is TwinGuard?

One platform. Every layer of facility intelligence.

TwinGuard is an AI-powered Digital Twin Platform that transforms static BIM and IFC building models into intelligent operational environments.

Rather than serving only as a BIM viewer, TwinGuard combines digital twin technology, facility intelligence, AI analytics, emergency planning, asset tracking, occupancy monitoring, operational visibility, and room-aware navigation into one connected system.

The platform creates a living representation of a facility where managers, operators, safety teams, maintenance teams, and occupants can understand spaces, locate assets, monitor operations, simulate emergencies, and make better decisions — all from one environment.

TwinGuard helps organizations move beyond static building models and toward intelligent facilities that are understandable, interactive, and actionable in real time.
