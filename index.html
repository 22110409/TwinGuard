The Problem

Why current BIM systems are difficult

Building Information Modeling has transformed how buildings are designed and constructed. But once a building is finished, the model that described it so precisely becomes almost impossible for anyone outside the engineering team to use. BIM and IFC viewers are built around geometry and coordinates, not around the people who actually have to operate, manage, or move through the building every day.

Traditional BIM Viewer

Built for engineers, not people

  • Raw geometry with no functional labels
  • Object names like IFCWALLSTANDARDCASE instead of "Room 204"
  • Navigation that requires CAD experience
  • Every wall, duct, and beam shown at once
  • No way to ask a simple question and get a simple answer
VS
TwinGuard AI

Built for understanding

  • Buildings organized into floors, rooms, and assets
  • Spaces labeled the way people actually refer to them
  • Click-to-explore navigation, no CAD background needed
  • Information surfaced only when it's relevant
  • Usable by facility staff, investors, and first-time visitors alike

The Solution

TwinGuard AI changes the experience

TwinGuard AI is a platform that converts BIM and IFC models into intelligent digital twins. It doesn't replace your model — it sits on top of it, reading the same geometry and data your design team already produced, and reorganizing it into a structure that matches how a building actually gets used and operated.

How TwinGuard Works

From raw model to living twin in six steps

TwinGuard follows the same pipeline for every building it processes, so the result stays consistent and accurate regardless of how complex the original model is.

01

Upload BIM / IFC Model

Bring in the model your design or engineering team already produced — no rebuilding or reformatting required.

02

Extract Floors & Rooms

TwinGuard reads the underlying geometry and identifies the building's floors, rooms, and boundaries automatically.

03

Classify Building Spaces

Each space is labeled by function — office, corridor, mechanical room — based on its layout and contents.

04

Generate Digital Twin

The classified spaces are assembled into a single interactive twin that visitors can navigate room by room.

05

Manage Operations

Assets, occupancy, and energy data are layered onto the twin so facility teams can track them in real time.

06

Monitor Safety & Emergencies

Exit routes and emergency scenarios are simulated against the twin, ready before they're ever needed in practice.

Project Vision

The future of intelligent facilities

Most buildings already have a BIM model sitting somewhere, unused once construction ends. TwinGuard AI is built on the belief that this model should keep working long after the building opens — as the foundation for how it's operated, maintained, and protected. The long-term goal is a digital twin that stays current as the building changes, flags problems before they escalate, and gives every person who relies on the building, not just the people who built it, a clear picture of where they are and what's happening around them.
